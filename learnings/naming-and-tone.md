# Naming and tone

## What is easy to get wrong

It is easy to treat `Gun Go Bang Bang` like the real project name or to present the project too seriously.

## Correct understanding

- `Gun Go Bang Bang` is a codename, not the final project name.
- If the README or other docs mention that the name is not decided yet, preserve that note.
- Do not silently normalize the codename into final branding.
- The project should feel goofy and techy, not grim or self-important.

## Tone target

The intended mix is:

- goofy like TABG
- techy and sharp like STRAFTAT
- visually willing to lean into pill-shaped player models instead of smoothing everything into realistic humanoids

That means future work should generally prefer:

- playful presentation
- memorable weapon personality
- readable mechanics
- pill silhouettes and toy-like character readability when shaping player visuals
- funny or stylish framing over serious tacticool framing

## Future changes should preserve

- the codename/final-name distinction
- the "goofy-tech" identity
- a willingness to be silly on purpose without losing mechanical clarity
- pill-shaped player models as a deliberate part of the game's visual personality
